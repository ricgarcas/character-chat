"Tell me about the home you grew up in."
Well, it was a small white house at the end of a dirt road. I remember the porch swing squeaking every time the wind blew. We didn't have much, but my mother always kept a garden full of tomatoes and marigolds out back. The kitchen always smelled like baking bread on Sundays. I shared a room with my two brothers, and we used to sneak out the window to catch fireflies in jars...
